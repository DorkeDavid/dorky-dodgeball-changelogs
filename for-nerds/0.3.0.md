Welcome to the technical breakdown of some new interesting mechanics I added in update 0.3.0! If you want to master animation canceling, calculate the exact timing of your shots, or just be a pro, this is for you.

### 1. COMBAT CANCEL MATRIX
This matrix dictates exactly how combat actions interact when triggered at the same time:

```text
               | New Action
---------------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------
Current Action | Dodge   | Throw   | Deflect | Catch   | Emote
---------------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------
Dodge          |   ---   | CANCEL  | BLOCKED | BLOCKED | CANCEL
Throw          | CANCEL  |   ---   | CANCEL  |   ---   | CANCEL
Deflect        | CANCEL  | CANCEL  |   ---   |   ---   | CANCEL
Catch          | CANCEL  | CANCEL  |   ---   |   ---   | CANCEL
Emote          | BLOCKED | BLOCKED | BLOCKED | BLOCKED |   —
```

* **CANCEL:** Your current action stops immediately to execute the new action.
* **BLOCKED:** The game ignores the input for the new action.
* **(---):** These actions do not directly overlap each other.

### 2. ACTION CONFIGURATIONS
This chart maps out your physical movement constraints, activation mechanics, and item requirements for every action:

```text
Action  | Input Type | Jump Disabled? | Walk Disabled? | Ball Required? | Length
--------+------------+----------------+----------------+----------------+--------
Dodge   | Hold       | YES            | YES            | ---            | ---
Throw   | Hold       | NO             | NO             | Equipped       | ---
Deflect | Tap        | NO             | NO             | Equipped       | 0.2s
Catch   | Hold       | YES            | NO             | Unequipped     | ---
Emote   | Toggle     | YES            | YES            | ---            | ---
Idle    | None       | NO             | NO             | ---            | ---
```

* **Ball State Rule:** Failing to satisfy the **Ball Required** state prevents you from starting that action. Furthermore, if you are actively performing an action and your ball state changes (like unequipping your ball mid-throw), the current action is immediately **CANCELED**.

### 3. THROW FORCES & CHARGE TIMELINE
Throwing force depends entirely on how long you hold down the input.

#### Throw Force Tiers
* **Base / Tap Throw:** 100
* **Power Shot:** 220
* **Max Force:** 320
* **Penalty Drop:** Drops immediately back to 100 Force

#### The Charge Timeline
* **0.0s – 0.2s ➔ Tap to Quick Charge**
    * Release at any point during this window for a **Base** throw. Holding past 0.2 seconds activates the **Quick Charge** phase (your ball turns pink!).
* **0.2s – 1.5s ➔ Rise to Power Shot**
    * Release anywhere in this window for a Quick Charge throw. Holding all the way to 1.5 seconds triggers a **Power Shot**.
* **1.5s – 2.5s ➔ Rise to Max Power**
    * Keeping the hold going past the initial Power Shot continues building velocity for 1 more second until you hit **Max Power**.
* **2.5s – 3.5s ➔ Plateau**
    * You can hold your absolute maximum force steady for up to 1 full second.
* **3.5s+ ➔ THE PENALTY**
    * If you attempt to hold max power for more than 1 second, the charge instantly breaks over a 0.2-second window. This triggers the **Penalty**, dropping you right back to base force in a poof of smoke!

### 4. MAP & GAMEMODE SELECTION WEIGHTS
Maps do not repeat, meaning you won’t see the same two maps in two different, concurrent options. I built a weight system to manage how often maps and modes show up.

```text
MAP ROTATION WEIGHTS          GAMEMODE WEIGHTS
• Classic:    50              • TwoTeams:  50
• SchoolGym:  50
• Crossroads: 30
```

* **How it works:** Instead of a completely blind random guess, the server reads these values to calculate selection probability. Higher weights mean a map/mode appears more frequently.
* *Note: Right now there is only one gamemode and three maps, so the weights won't change the rotation anyway, but they will be really handy for future updates.*
