Performance, gameplay, security, have improved. I took a massive undertaking in remodeling most of my old code. Still some more to remodel, coming in the next update.
### Security & Performance
* **Loading Speed:** The game now loads significantly faster.
* **Exploit Prevention:** Massive security overhaul to block cheaters, rigorously check arguments, and rate-limit remote event spam.
* **Server Latency Fix:** Match countdowns now dynamically account for server lag.
### Combat
* **New Action Matrix:** Added a cancel matrix system to handle concurrent actions.
  * *Mobile Players:* Buttons will dynamically hide/show based on your current action state.
  * *Desktop Players:* Inputs will be blocked.
* **Throwing:**
  * Throws now have a longer charge, but power shots force has been buffed a lot.
  * Overall, throws have more physical force but a slightly slower travel time.
  * Added a **Quick Charge** mechanic (turns the ball pink with a cool charge SFX!).
  * **Power Shot:** The fire effects now tween, the charge bar is now vertical on the right side, and there is a smaller target size.
  * **Smart Camera Tracking:** The power shot zoom-in/out now saves your target zoom, meaning it properly accounts for past camera obstructions without breaking.
  * **The Penalty System:** Holding a power shot too long leads to a PENALTY! You will drop right back to base force in a poof of smoke and a poof SFX.
  * You can now unequip your dodgeball at absolutely any time to cancel a throw.
* **Balance Fixes:** Removed the deflect cooldown entirely. Attempting to catch is now instantly canceled the second you equip a dodgeball. Every single action animation is now handled in the Combat service.
### Round & Map
* **Map Spawning:** Maps are now cleanly cloned straight into the workspace when needed, instead of sitting spaced out super far apart in the distance.
* **Smart Spawn Pads:** Dodgeballs now spawn with spacing, compactness, can overflow on the Y-axis if needed, and split evenly on each side of the halfline barriers.
* **Dynamic Halfline:** The halfline barrier is now a solid white beam that physically blocks your movement instead of instantly killing you.
* **Dodgeball Reload Pads:** Reload pads on big maps (like Crossroads) have been converted into plain collision barriers.
* **Better Voting Options:** Adjusted the code to support completely new submodules for custom gamemodes and events. Added dynamic map/gamemode weighting.
* **Input Type Upgrade:** Input tracking now uses the more accurate `UserInputService.PreferredInput.`
### Sudden Death
* **Smooth Visuals:** The old jittery/flickering part circle has been replaced with linearly tweening white beams.
* **Team Catch-Up:** If active due to team imbalance, the sudden death system will highlight all remaining players on the losing team for 3 seconds.
### Visuals & Quality of Life
* **UI Clean Up:** The top bar during an active round now explicitly shows your selected option (Gamemode \| Map) and formats the match time cleanly in minutes:seconds (MM:SS). It also shows the current round state, (like intermission).
* **Smart Notifications:** Added a clean scrolling buffer (max 2 concurrent notifications at the very top). Sudden death notifications have been moved to the top bar, and you will now get a top bar warning if you try to catch with a full inventory.
* **Emotipops:** Cooldown is now immediate—start a new one the millisecond your last one ends!
* **Sound Setting Fix:** Adjusting your sound effects setting will no longer accidentally mute default Roblox game sounds.
* **Menu Upgrades:** Menu swiping transitions are now faster.
* **Fixed:** The default emote animation has as much energy now as it should have lol.
* **THE BIG FIX:** Finally fixed that bug where the camera spawned facing the wrong direction.
### Temporary Disables (Returning Soon!)

* The Lobby Dummy doesn’t throw.
* Muted the dodgeball hit (bell), death, and emotipop sound effects.
* Hidden the menu Tix display.
